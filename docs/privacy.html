Privacy Policy

How SceneFace handles your recordings, permissions, purchase status, and support requests.

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how SceneFace and the support site at sceneface-app.newtree.tech handle information when you use the app, contact support, or read these pages.

2. Information processed by SceneFace

  • Recordings, subtitle text, export files, and edit notes created in the app.
  • Permission states for camera, microphone, speech recognition, and photo-library access.
  • Purchase, entitlement, and restore status used to determine whether SceneFace Pro is unlocked.
  • Information you send when you email support.

3. How the app uses permissions

  • Camera and microphone access are required to record video with audio.
  • Speech Recognition access is optional and is used only when automatic subtitles are enabled.
  • Photo Library Add Only access is optional and is used only when you save an export to Photos.

4. Speech recognition

When you enable automatic subtitles and grant Speech Recognition permission, SceneFace sends live audio buffers to Apple’s speech recognition framework to generate captions. SceneFace is configured to allow network-backed recognition when on-device recognition is unavailable.

5. Purchases and entitlements

Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. SceneFace uses Apple’s StoreKit framework and RevenueCat services to load the lifetime unlock product, complete purchases, restore purchases, and confirm whether SceneFace Pro is unlocked on this device.

6. How information is used

  • To capture video, create subtitles, render exports, and save files on your device.
  • To determine whether SceneFace Pro features are available to your account.
  • To respond to support requests and troubleshoot reported issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce app terms when required.

7. Sharing

  • Apple may process purchase, speech recognition, and photo-library operations you trigger in the app.
  • RevenueCat may process purchase identifiers, entitlement status, and restore events needed to support SceneFace Pro.
  • Content is shared with others only when you explicitly export or share it.
  • Information may be disclosed if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

SceneFace does not sell your personal information.

8. Storage and retention

  • Recordings, subtitle files, and exports are stored locally on your device or in your chosen photo library.
  • Purchase records are retained by Apple under Apple’s own policies.
  • Support emails are retained as long as reasonably necessary to respond, follow up, and keep support history.

9. Your choices

  • You can disable automatic subtitles in the app at any time.
  • You can revoke permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can use Restore Purchases in the app to recover a previous SceneFace Pro lifetime unlock on the same Apple account.
  • Billing, refunds, and account-level purchase controls are handled by Apple under Apple’s policies.
  • You can delete recordings and exports from your device or photo library.

10. Changes and contact

This policy may be updated as SceneFace changes. Updated versions will be posted on this site. For privacy questions, email support@sceneface-app.newtree.tech.
