Technical Support

Technical support for SceneFace, purchase help, and legal information in one place.

SceneFace records the front and back cameras together on supported iPhones, lets you choose Performance or Flexible Export, supports live subtitles, and exports one finished video with burned-in captions in vertical or horizontal framing. Use this page as the app's technical support destination for troubleshooting, purchase help, privacy details, and terms of use.

Contact support

Email support@sceneface-app.newtree.tech. To speed things up, include your iPhone model, iOS version, app version, what you expected to happen, and whether the issue involves subtitles, export, permissions, or SceneFace Pro purchases.

Current app behavior

Recording modes

Performance mode saves a finished video right away. Flexible Export captures source footage first, then renders the final video after the take.

Front camera layouts

You can turn the front camera off, use Picture in Picture, or switch to Split Screen. PiP also supports size, shape, and position changes.

Live subtitles

Speech Recognition is optional. When enabled, SceneFace can generate live subtitles and you can review and edit finalized subtitle text before export.

SceneFace Pro

SceneFace Pro is a one-time lifetime unlock handled by Apple. The current paid difference is watermark-free saved videos. There is no subscription.

Frequently asked questions

Why are live subtitles missing?

Check that Automatic Subtitles is on in Settings and that Speech Recognition permission is granted. Recording still works when subtitles are off.

How do I change the front camera layout?

Open Settings to turn the front camera off, switch between Picture in Picture and Split Screen, or adjust PiP size and shape.

Why can’t I save to Photos?

SceneFace needs Photo Library Add Only access when you save an export to Photos. If saving is unavailable, you can still share the exported file directly.

How do I restore SceneFace Pro?

Open the in-app Settings page and use Restore Purchases. SceneFace Pro is a lifetime unlock, so there is no subscription to manage or cancel.

Before you email support

  • Confirm whether the issue happens on every recording or only on one specific take.
  • Note whether camera, microphone, speech recognition, or photo permissions were denied.
  • For subtitle issues, mention whether Automatic Subtitles was enabled and whether the recording reached the 60-second subtitle cap.
  • For purchase issues, include the Apple ID region and whether you already tried Restore Purchases.
  • For export issues, mention whether you used Performance mode or Flexible Export mode and whether the front camera was on.
