Technical Support

Help for SceneFace, billing questions, and legal information in one place.

SceneFace records dual-camera video on supported iPhones, adds live subtitles when speech recognition is enabled, and exports portrait videos with burned-in captions. Use this page for support requests, privacy details, and terms of use.

Contact support

Email support@sceneface-app.newtree.tech. To speed things up, include your iPhone model, iOS version, app version, what you expected to happen, and whether the issue involves subtitles, export, permissions, or SceneFace Pro purchases.

Common help topics

Device compatibility

Dual-camera capture requires AVCaptureMultiCamSession. SceneFace is intended for iPhone XS-class devices and newer.

Permissions

Camera and microphone are required. Speech Recognition is optional and only used when you enable automatic subtitles.

Subscriptions

SceneFace Pro billing is handled through Apple. If your purchase does not unlock, open Settings and use Restore Purchases.

Exports

SceneFace exports portrait video. Front-camera preview is mirrored while recording, but exports use an unmirrored front-camera result.

Frequently asked questions

Why are live subtitles missing?

Check that Automatic Subtitles is on in Settings and that Speech Recognition permission is granted. Recording still works without subtitles.

Why can’t I save to Photos?

SceneFace needs Photo Library Add Only access when you explicitly save an export. You can also share the exported file without saving to Photos.

How do I manage or restore SceneFace Pro?

Open the in-app Settings page, then use Manage Subscription, Open Customer Center, or Restore Purchases depending on your account state.

What should I send in a bug report?

Include the device model, iOS version, app version, steps to reproduce, any error text shown on screen, and whether the issue happens every time.

Before you email support

  • Confirm whether the issue happens on every recording or only on one specific take.
  • Note whether camera, microphone, speech recognition, or photo permissions were denied.
  • For purchase issues, include the Apple ID region and whether you already tried Restore Purchases.
  • For export issues, mention whether you used Performance mode or Flexible Export mode.
